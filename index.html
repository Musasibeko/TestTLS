How It Works

Four Steps. Full Protection.

Transport-Link creates a verified, documented trail from booking to delivery. Every party is accountable at every step.

01

Customer Books

Submit your transport request with pickup location, destination, cargo details and contact info. The system generates your unique credentials instantly.

→ Trip ID + Pickup PIN generated
02

Driver Verifies

Only pre-verified drivers can accept jobs. Every driver has submitted ID, license, vehicle details and photos before joining the platform.

→ Verified Driver assigned
03

PIN Handover

At pickup, the driver enters the PIN you received. This confirms the correct driver collected the correct goods. No PIN match, no departure.

→ Pickup confirmed on record
04

Delivery Confirmed

At dropoff, the driver submits the Trip ID, receiver name, delivery PIN, photo proof and timestamp. This becomes your official proof of delivery.

→ Delivery receipt generated
Security Features

Built Around Accountability

Every feature exists to protect the customer, the driver, and the business.

🔐

PIN-Secured Handovers

Every job generates a unique pickup PIN. Goods only leave with a driver who can verify the correct code. This one step eliminates the majority of fake pickup fraud.

🪪

Driver Verification

Every driver on the platform has been screened. ID, driver's license, truck registration and photos are all on record. No verification, no access to jobs.

📸

Photo Proof of Delivery

Drivers submit a timestamped photo at delivery. This is attached to the trip record permanently. No more "I delivered it" with no evidence.

📋

Official Delivery Records

Every completed trip generates a tamper-evident record containing: Trip ID, driver ID, timestamps, pickup PIN confirmation, receiver name, delivery photo and payment status.

  • Trip ID linked to original booking
  • Driver identity confirmed at every stage
  • Timestamp of pickup and delivery
  • Receiver name collected at dropoff
  • Photo and payment confirmation attached
